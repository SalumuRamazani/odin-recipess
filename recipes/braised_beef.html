Braised Beef

This braised beef recipe is a classic dish that is perfect for a comforting meal.
The beef is cooked slowly in a flavorful broth until it is tender and juicy, and the vegetables are cooked to perfection. The result is a hearty and flavorful meal that is sure to please everyone at the table.

be ready to eat a lot

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef chuck roast
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the beef to the bowl and toss to coat.
  4. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  5. Add the beef and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
