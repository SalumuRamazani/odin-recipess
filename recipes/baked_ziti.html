Baked Ziti

Baked ziti is a classic Italian dish that is made with spaghetti squash, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce.
The dish is baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the spaghetti squash is tender. It is typically served with a generous amount of marinara sauce on top for added flavor.

be ready to eat a lot

Ingredients

  • 1 box ziti pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook the ziti pasta according to the package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked ziti, marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and sprinkle with bread crumbs.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
