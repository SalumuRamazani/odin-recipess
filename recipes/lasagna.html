Lasagna

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

be ready to eat a lot

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/2 cup minced garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 2 cans tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium-high heat until browned, about 5 minutes. Add onion and garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
